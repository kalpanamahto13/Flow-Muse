# Flow-Muse
FlowMuse is a simple yet smart web-based period tracker. It helps users log their menstrual cycles, track moods and symptoms, and get cycle predictions through an intuitive, calendar-based interface. The app aims to make menstrual tracking effortless and empowering-promoting self-awareness and wellness through clean design and useful insights.  

## Live website link 
- https://kalpanamahto13.github.io/Flow-Muse/

## ✨ Features

* **Creative Writing Space** – Write poems, thoughts, stories, or notes.
* **Idea Flow Tracker** – Track how ideas evolve over time.
* **Daily Journaling** – Maintain a personal journal with timestamps.
* **Mood-Based Themes** – Switch between multiple aesthetic themes.
* **Autosave** – Never lose your work.
* **Category Organizer** – Create folders for poems, notes, drafts, and more.
* **Export Options** – Download your writing as text or PDF.
* **Search System** – Quickly find any past entry.
